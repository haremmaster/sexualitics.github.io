Sexualitics tries to contribute to human sexuality understanding through a Big Data approach, starting with releasing datasets for quantitative analysis of online pornography.

Stay informed of dataset, analysis and visualisations updates through our Twitter account. If you have additionnal or derivated dataset you would like to share, let us now, we would be glad to put them here. Same for visualisations, analysis or else.

This project is born and supported at Fabelier, a hackerspace in Paris. Fabelier's mailing-list might be a place to start interacting around this project.
